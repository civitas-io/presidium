## Adoption Reality

### The Good

- **78% of Fortune 500** have deployed or are testing agents (2026 projected)
- **40% of enterprise applications** will feature task-specific agents by end of 2026 (Gartner)
- **74% achieve ROI within first year** for those who reach production (Google Cloud)
- **171% average ROI** for organizations running agents in production

### The Bad

- **88% of AI agents fail to reach production** (TURION.AI, 2026)
- **40% of agentic AI projects will be canceled by 2027** (Gartner)
- **Only 2% of organizations** have achieved scaled deployment (RAYSolute)
- **Only 11% of pilots** transition to full production (Tactical Edge AI)
- **90-95% of agent pilots** never reach production (NeuralWired)
- **65% of leaders** cite agentic system complexity as top barrier (KPMG)

### The Ugly

- **Only 24.4%** have full visibility into agent-to-agent communication (Gravitee)
- **Over half of all agents** run without security oversight or logging
- **Only 21%** have mature governance models for agents (Deloitte)
- **73% of leaders** cite security and data privacy as top concerns (Deloitte)
- **$890,000** average implementation cost for enterprise agent systems
